# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use and protect your personal information.

## 1. Information we collect

We collect the information you provide during registration, including company name, contact details, commercial registration documents, VAT certificates, bank information and other documents required for supplier qualification.

## 2. How we use your information

Your information is used to verify your company identity, qualify you as an approved supplier, process procurement transactions and communicate with you regarding RFQs, contracts and regulatory compliance.

## 3. Sharing information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your information may be shared with authorised procurement team members, auditors and regulators where legally required.

## 4. Data security

We apply security standards to protect your information, including encryption of sensitive documents, secure access controls and regular security audits.

## 5. Retention of documents

Business and registration documents are retained for at least seven years for audit compliance and as required by applicable law and regulations.

## 6. Your rights

You have the right to access, update or request deletion of your personal information by contacting our procurement team.

## 7. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries please contact the procurement department.

---

This portal complies with the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL).
